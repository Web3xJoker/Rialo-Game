WTF is Rialo?

Rialo isn’t a layer one. It is a next-generation blockchain designed to seamlessly bridge Web2 and Web3 by solving real-world problems that existing chains have ignored for years.

The Goal

Rialo removes all the daily crypto pain—no more clunky wallets, seed phrases, or confusing interfaces.

It blends Web3 into the apps you already use, so blockchain just works in your everyday life.

Enjoy all the benefits - speed, security, and ownership; with zero hassle or friction.

Funding

Rialo recently announced a $20M Subzerolabs initial raise led by Pantera Capital to build the chain. It is also to be noted that Rialo is being backed by other blockchain pioneers including Variant, Hashed, Fabric Ventures, Coinbase Ventures, Mirana, Mysten Labs, Susquehanna, Edge Ventures, Flowdesk and others.
